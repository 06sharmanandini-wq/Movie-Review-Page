Mahavatar Narsimha

The demon Hiranyakashipu, Hiranyaksha’s brother, gains a boon from Brahma that makes him nearly invincible. He declares himself a god and persecutes Vishnu’s devotees, including his own son Prahlad. To protect Prahlad and uphold dharma, Vishnu manifests as Narsimha, a half-man, half-lion form, and defeats Hiranyakashipu in a dramatic, symbolic battle between faith and arrogance
